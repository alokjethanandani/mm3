strategy requires making.

True innovation emerges from chaos.
The only way to understand your target audience is to create.

The only way to get out of a red ocean is to go where no one has gone before. Unfortunately, traditional strategy and planning don’t work there. You can’t size a market that doesn’t exist yet. You can’t examine competitors when they haven’t yet launched. To truly innovate, I validate demand by testing your idea with your target audience.
